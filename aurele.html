The Process

Slow work.

We don't manufacture. We make. Every piece goes through the same four hands, in the same order, every time.

I
Sketch & Intent

Every design starts with a feeling, not a trend. We sketch until the shape holds something real.

II
Wax Carving

The form is carved by hand in wax — slowly, over days. No shortcuts. No digital shortcuts.

III
Cast & Finish

Lost-wax casting in recycled silver or gold. Then hours of hand-finishing to the right texture.

IV
Your Piece Ships

Wrapped in cotton, sealed with wax. Yours within 5–8 days of ordering. No warehouse. No stock.

Questions We Hear

We'd ask the same things.

?
Will it tarnish quickly?
We only use .925 sterling silver and solid 14k gold — not plated. With basic care, it lasts decades. We include a care card with every order.
?
What if my size isn't right?
We'll resize it for free within 60 days. Most rings ship with a half-size up option. If it still doesn't fit, we'll remake it.
?
How long does shipping take?
5–8 business days from order to door — US, Europe, and Gulf. We make to order, so there's a short wait. Worth it, we promise.
?
Is it really handmade?
Yes. We make everything in our studio in Portland. You can see the process in our Instagram stories — every week, live and unedited.
